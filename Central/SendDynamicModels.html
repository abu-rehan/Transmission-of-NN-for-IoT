Transferring a Neural Network over BLE

Transfer a Hello World Model using this UI. You can transfer a sine, a triangle or a square model.

  • Open Chrome's console to see the status.
  • Connect to the device by clicking on the 'Connect' button, choosing the device and pairing.
  • When connection has established, choose and send the model.
